A Travel Agency Analysis is a data-driven evaluation of a travel agency's bookings, customer preferences, agent performance, and revenue streams. In the travel and tourism industry, profit margins heavily depend on seasonality, destination popularity, and marketing efficiency.

When conducting an analysis for a travel agency (like the project you are uploading for your Travel-Agency-Analysis repository), you are turning raw booking logs and customer inquiries into actionable insights to increase bookings and optimize tour packages.
